My story

Hi, my name is Audrey Alexander and I run a small business where I make custom Jewelry. Since I was a kid I have loved jewelry and how it made me feel. I remember playing dress-up with my mother and how excited I would get when I put on the final piece of jewelry, It made the outfit feel complete. My goal is to create pieces that make people have that same excitement I had as a little girl playing dress-up with her mom.

My Work

I create patterns for rings, necklaces, bracelets, and other accessories. As Well as custom pieces for any occasions.
